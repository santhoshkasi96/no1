How SSP spiced up India’s Food Industry

From a local secret to a national spice sensation, the SSP brand has enabled asafoetida to find its place in India’s booming food flavoring market. Here’s how the Indian market for branded, quality Asafoetida was created and grew, from the early 50s till today, thanks to SSP. Here’s the genesis of the SSP brand of hing and it’s illustrious founder SS Pandian

1930

S S Pandian was born and raised in a businessman’s family in a small town called Kamuthi, in Madurai District, Tamil Nadu.

1946

When his father’s business faced hurdles, the 16-year old SS Pandian left his home town, traveling to Chennai and Mumbai seeking opportunities. There his knowledge of asafoetida, also known as hingu and perungayam grew.

The early 50s

Asafoetida was sold loose by weight; people were unaware about quality.

1957

This was an eventful year for the 27-year old Sri. S S Pandian. He moved to Bangalore with Rs.2/- in his pocket and a dream in his heart - to create quality, easy-to-use asafoetida powder and paste.

The same year, SS Pandian launched two asafoetida variants that soon gained consumer confidence: Pre-packed quality Compounded Asafoetida, priced at one Anna per packet - a first in Karnataka. Medium (now Crystal) Compounded Asafoetida - a first in the country.

1962

Special Compounded Asafoetida was launched.

1986

Microfine Compounded Asafoetida was launched.

1987 - 2003

These years saw the launch of:

  • PN Compounded Asafoetida, a bigger granulated variety
  • Hotel Special Asafoetida, exclusively to enhance hotel food
  • 303 Compounded Asafoetida, for premium and corporate kitchens
  • 4-in-1 Value Pack, a tester pack with four varieties of Asafoetida

With the awareness created by SS Pandian, customers are now able to buy the Asafoetida variant of their choice. Quality, customer satisfaction and appreciation continue to be the hallmarks of the brand wherever in the country or overseas, great taste and aroma is appreciated and sought after.

This in essence, is how the SSP brand rose from obscurity to stardom, as Asafoetida emerged as a key player in India’s spice trade. Consumers today get value for money, every time they buy authentic Asafoetida from SSP.
