About Us

It’s always the little things that make a big difference in life. Small alterations lead to sweeping transformations. In Indian cooking, there are numerous ingredients which are integral to providing the right balance taste and flavor. But, none of the spices can match the subtle omnipotence of asafoetida. SSP asafoetida had produced a stellar reputation in the market for producing premium quality asafoetida that continues to cast a magic spell upon the numerous Indian cuisines.

BACK TO THE ROOTS

Sri S. S. Pandian was born and raised in a business-class family in Kamuthi, a small town in South India. During his childhood, when his father’s business faced hurdles, the teenager, aged 16, left his home with an ambition to start a business of his own. He travelled to Chennai and Mumbai looking for the right opportunity. There, he gained a little knowledge about asafoetida, also known as hing, hingu or perungayam in India.

BACK TO THE ROOTS

During early 1950’s Asafoetida was sold in loose by weighment just like any other grocery items. People did not know much about the quality of Asafoetida. For the first time in Karnataka in 1957, Sri. S.S. Pandian introduced to the market quality Compounded Asafoetida in packets with a price tag of one Anna per packet. This made people realise the superior quality of Compounded Asafoetida. For the first time in India in 1957 Sri. S.S. Pandian introduced the first of its kind in the country SSP Medium (now Crystal) Compounded Asafoetida in 1957, SSP Special Compounded Asafoetida in 1962, and SSP Microfine Compounded Asafoetida in 1986. In short, three different varieties of Asafoetida each with unique taste were introduced to the market and Sri. S.S. Pandian .

deserves exclusive credit for this invention. With the awareness created by Sri. S.S. Pandian, customers are now able to buy the required variety, viz., Medium, Special and Powder to tickle their taste buds. Since introduction, these products continue to enjoy customer satisfaction and appreciation. The quality of S.S.P Asafoetida is maintained at all times and the consumer gets value for money every time they buy S.S.P. products.
